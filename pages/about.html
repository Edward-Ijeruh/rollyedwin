About Us

RollyEdwin Integrated Services Ltd. is a leading provider of integrated solutions in architecture & real estate, interior design, solar street lighting, cleaning & fumigation, and table water services. Our commitment to excellence and innovation drives us to deliver exceptional results for our clients.

Our Story

Founded in 2005, RollyEdwin Integrated Services Ltd. began with a vision to transform spaces and enhance lives through innovative and sustainable solutions. Over the years, we have grown from a small startup to a reputable company, known for our quality, reliability, and customer-centric approach. Our journey has been marked by continuous learning, adaptation, and a relentless pursuit of excellence.

Our Mission

Bringing your building designs and finishing imagination to reality, steady electricity, natural water with good environment through cleaning and fumigation, car sales and general contractors.

Our Vision

Client satisfaction through provision of reliable services and products whilst assuring honesty, integrity, quality and affordability.

Our Team

Our team comprises experienced professionals with diverse expertise in architecture, interior design, engineering, and service management. We foster a collaborative environment where creativity and innovation thrive, ensuring that every project benefits from a wealth of knowledge and skills.
